1:1Process

Akaris was born from a simple belief: that the objects around you should be as distinctive as you are. Every shelf, every wall, every desk corner is an opportunity to say something.

We work directly with clients — cafés, homes, businesses, creators — to design and produce pieces that belong nowhere else. There is no warehouse, no catalogue, no run of thousands. Only pieces made with intent.

Based in Hyderabad, we serve clients across India. Whether you have a precise brief or just a vague feeling, we will help you find the form.
